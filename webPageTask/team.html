Alexander Sergeev Martinov

Alexander Martinov is an award winning freelance writer, journalist, and author with a passion for telling stories about social justice and environmental issues. He is currently an MFA student in Creative Writing at the University of British Columbia. His essay Herd Memory won a National Magazine Award in June 2020 in the Personal Journalism category.
